  • P3: The Box Dimension

    • By Braden Burns, Nikki Hess, Ryder Spees, Oliver Pardi, and Anthony Vanlian

    • Play the game in your browser! (itch.io)

    • My role on P3:

    • I worked on a team of 5 students, managing a single repository and incorporating feedback from several playtests.

    • I was the primary engine tool developer, 3D animator, and marketing lead on this Unity Game:

      • Engine Tool Developer: I wrote the underlying C# scripting for the core game: level loading, grid-based logic, as well as abstract implementations of player and enemy movement.

      • 3D Animator: I produced the animations of the cat, rat, dog, and robot enemies (which I created in Blender then imported to Unity).

      • Marketing: I published the game to four websites (IndieDB, TIGSource, Itchi, and GameJolt) alongside text blurbs, images, and a video trailer produced by fellow team members.
