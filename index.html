The Box Dimension

3D Isometric Turn-based Action-Adventure

Play as a cat having an imaginative adventure inside a series of cardboard boxes!

  • Play in Browser
  • Trailer

  • By Braden Burns, Nikki Hess, Ryder Spees, Oliver Pardi, and Anthony Vanlian

  • Game engine: Unity

  • I was the lead engine tool developer, 3D animator, and marketer:

    • Engine Tool Developer: I wrote the underlying C# scripting for the core game: level data types, level transitions, grid logic, as well as abstract implementations of player and enemy movement.

    • 3D Animator: I animated and integrated the rat, dog, robot, and cat (except for the cat walk cycle) characters.

    • Marketing: I published the game to multiple websites (IndieDB, TIGSource, Itch.io, and GameJolt) alongside promotional text, images, and a video trailer produced by fellow team members.
